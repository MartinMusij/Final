Argentina!

Argentina is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. With an extremely diverse landscape and a culture to match it, there are endless attractions to experience!

History!

Prehistory in the present territory of Argentina began with the first human settlements on the southern tip of Patagonia around 13,000 years ago. Written history began with the arrival of Spanish chroniclers in 1516, which marks the beginning of Spanish domination in this region.

Culture!

The culture of Argentina is as varied as its geography. The large amount of ethnic groups that compose the country make it rich in culture. Modern culture is greatly influence by Europoean, Asian, and African immigration

Landscape!

Argentina is the second largest country in South America. Due to its' enormous size, the country has different climates and landscapes. You can find woodlands, deserts, wetlands, lakes, waterfalls, mountains, and the extremely famous southern Glaciers.
